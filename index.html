Popular Domestic Destinations

Goa

International and domestic tourists visit this tiny state every year in large numbers. Tourists are attracted to its golden beaches, architecture, and places of worship. It is a biodiversity hotspot and is rich in flora and fauna.

Odisha

Odisha, located on India’s eastern coast, is a culturally rich state known for its ancient temples, pristine beaches, and vibrant festivals. It is home to the majestic Konark Sun Temple, the sacred Jagannath Temple in Puri, and the serene Chilika Lake, Asia's largest brackish water lagoon.

Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh, the heart of India, is a treasure trove of heritage and nature. It boasts UNESCO World Heritage sites like Khajuraho and Sanchi, majestic forts, and ancient temples. Wildlife lovers flock to Kanha and Bandhavgarh National Parks.

Popular International Destinations

Dubai

Dubai is one of the world's leading tourism destinations. In 2018, it was the fourth most-visited city globally based on international visitor numbers.

Singapore

Singapore is a famous island city-state known for its modern skyline, efficient transport, and cultural blend. It is a global hub for trade, tourism, and innovation.

Bali

Bali is one of Indonesia’s top tourist destinations, known for its beautiful beaches, temples, culture, and welcoming hospitality.
